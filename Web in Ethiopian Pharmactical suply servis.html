With over seventy years of service,
the Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service (EPSS) is
a veteran institution in providing pharmaceuticals,
laboratory reagents, medical equipment and supplies to
the Ethiopian people. Despite the numerous changes
in nomenclature throughout the years,
supplying pharmaceuticals has always been our core activity.

HELLO, do you know Ethiopian Pharmaceuticals Supply Service-EPSS

Supporting local manufacturing to build their capacity is one of EPSS’s strategic initiatives. EPSS; by now working for the implementation of the support by collaborating with MOF,MOH, PPA and other stakeholders. The local Manufactures will have up to 55% of the foreign currency facilitation support but they shall submit their product input-output coefficient to Armawer Hansen Research Institute (AHRI) for the analysis. Otherwise, EPSS cannot give this privilege. For those who did not submit the same, still they can participate and compute in any tenders. EPSS will not take any responsibility for the loss opportunities of the manufacturers.
